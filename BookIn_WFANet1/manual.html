Welcome to BookIn!

BookIn is a records management system to help BookIn Bookship go through their administrative tasks effortlessly. This user manual will guide you through the main features and interfaces of BookIn.

Main Interfaces

Interface 1: Managing Books

The Books interface allows you to browse and control the information about books in the system including isbn, title, publication year, author, etc. You can easily track the inventory of books and update book details.

Interface 2: Managing Orders

The Orders interface helps you keep track of the orders that the bookshop receives. You can check the books that are sold most and the quantity they are ordering the books in, the customer who ordered, the amount of the order, the date the order was placed, and whether the order was delivered or not yet.

Interface 3: Managing Customers

The Customers interface provides personal information over the history of customers who had ordered in BookIn. It shows their address for the shipping, basic details such as names, and contact details such as phone number. It also shows how much the customer has spent over all their orders in total to let the admin know about their purchasing power.

Interface 4: Managing Admins

The Admin interface allows you to view and manage other BookIn admins. It displays the individuals who have admin access and their credentials to BookIn. The passwords are only in hash value for security purposes.

General

For all the interfaces listed above, there are similar functionalities such as the ability to add, update, and delete details in the system, the ability to import and export the data in csv format, and the ability to import the data to excel for printing purposes.
